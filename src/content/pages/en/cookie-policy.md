# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

This policy explains exactly which cookies **giorgiosaud.io** sets, who sets them, and how long they last.

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They allow websites to remember information about your visit.

---

## Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the Site to function and cannot be disabled.

| Name | Provider | Purpose | Expiry |
|------|----------|---------|--------|
| `cookie_consent` | giorgiosaud.io | Stores your cookie consent choice | 12 months |
| `better-auth.session_token` | giorgiosaud.io | Keeps you logged in if you have an account | Session / 30 days |
| `better-auth.csrf_token` | giorgiosaud.io | Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks | Session |

### Analytics Cookies (optional — requires consent)

These cookies help me understand how visitors use the Site. They are only set after you give explicit consent.

| Name | Provider | Purpose | Expiry |
|------|----------|---------|--------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics 4 | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics 4 | Session state for GA4 | 2 years |

Google Analytics is configured with `anonymize_ip: true` — your IP address is anonymised before storage.

### No Marketing Cookies

This Site does not use any advertising or marketing cookies.

## Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics (GA4) may set cookies from the `google-analytics.com` domain. These are only activated with your consent. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

---

## How to Control Cookies

**On this Site:** Use the **🍪 Cookies** button at the bottom of the page to review or change your consent at any time.

**In your browser:** You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

Note: blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect Site functionality.
